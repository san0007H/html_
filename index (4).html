Who We Are

Healing Hands, Compassionate Care

Dr. Pandurang Magar is a distinguished orthopaedic surgeon with over 15 years of dedicated service in bone, joint, and musculoskeletal medicine. His commitment to patient-centric care and surgical precision has earned him the trust of thousands across the region.

Trained at premier medical institutions, Dr. Magar combines evidence-based medicine with the latest minimally invasive surgical techniques to deliver outcomes that give patients their life back.

His philosophy is simple — every patient deserves personalized attention, honest communication, and the highest standard of orthopaedic care available.

  • MBBS  Bachelor of Medicine & Bachelor of Surgery
  • MS Ortho  Master of Surgery in Orthopaedics
  • Fellow  Advanced Joint Replacement Surgery
  • Member  Indian Orthopaedic Association (IOA)

Our Specialties

Joint Replacement

🦴
Spine Surgery
Comprehensive management of spinal disorders including disc prolapse, spondylosis, and minimally invasive spine stabilisation.
⚡
Sports Medicine
Diagnosis and treatment of sports-related injuries including ACL tears, rotator cuff injuries, and ligament reconstruction.
🦷
Trauma & Fractures
Expert management of complex fractures, dislocations, and polytrauma cases using modern fixation techniques.
🔬
Arthroscopy
Minimally invasive keyhole surgeries for knee, shoulder, and ankle conditions ensuring faster recovery and smaller scars.
💊
Paediatric Orthopaedics
Specialised care for children's bone and joint conditions including clubfoot, limb deformities, and growth plate injuries.
